Underground Surveys



Accurate Utility Mapping Below Ground


At 3D Services, we provide comprehensive underground utility surveys using advanced detection equipment, including dual-frequency Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR) and Electromagnetic Locators (EML). This allows us to detect and map both metallic and non-metallic underground services — even in complex or high-traffic environments.

PAS 128 Compliant Utility Surveys


  • Level D: Desktop utility record search
  • Level C: Validation through visual inspection
  • Level B: Detection using EML and/or GPR (non-intrusive)
  • Level A: Confirmed by physical excavation or exposure

Minimise Risk, Maximise Safety


By identifying underground infrastructure before construction begins, our utility surveys help prevent costly damage, reduce health and safety risks, and improve site planning efficiency — especially on urban or redevelopment sites.
