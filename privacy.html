Privacy Policy

At 3D Services, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy outlines how we handle personal data collected through this website.


What We Collect


We do not use cookies or collect personal data automatically when you visit our website.

If you choose to contact us via our website form, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your message or inquiry

How We Use Your Information


Your information is used solely for the purpose of responding to your message. We do not share, sell, or rent your personal data to third parties.


Form Submissions


Messages submitted through our contact form are securely processed by Formsubmit and sent directly to our email inbox. We do not store your data on our website or host servers.


Your Rights


If you would like us to delete any message or information you submitted through our contact form, please contact us at enquiries@3dservices.co.uk.


Contact


If you have any questions about this privacy policy, feel free to reach out using the contact details provided on our website.
