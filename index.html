Company Profile


With over 40 years of industry experience, 3D delivers tailored surveying solutions to meet each client's specific needs and budget.

Based in the Surrey Hills, our agile team serves the South East with minimal travel time — maximizing time on-site to provide expert support and accurate results.

Our CAD specialists transform survey data into high-quality drawings and 3D models. We stay ahead with the latest equipment from Leica and Trimble.

By continuously evaluating new technologies, we ensure efficient, cost-effective services across surveying, construction management, and planning consultancy.
