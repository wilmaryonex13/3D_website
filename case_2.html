Engineering Surveys and Setting Out in Thorpe Park

Our team was engaged to provide a range of engineering surveys in support of the construction of a major new roller coaster at a popular amusement park in Chertsey. The project required high-precision data to guide both planning and on-site activities for the new ride, known as Hyperia. Working closely with the construction team, our surveyors were tasked with mapping key infrastructure and providing reliable coordinates for setting out structural elements.

Using the GEOMAX Zoom95 robotic total station, we carried out multiple survey operations across the site. These included a full drainage as-built survey to capture the location and condition of subsurface utilities, as well as a detailed survey of the adjacent lake boundary and the outline of proposed infill areas. These elements were essential in informing the site's drainage strategy and environmental planning requirements.

In parallel with the infrastructure survey, our team completed an as-laid survey of newly installed electric cabling routes. This ensured that buried services were properly documented for future maintenance and integration. We also performed topographic surveys of the broader construction zones, generating precise data for volume calculations. These calculations played a key role in material costing and resource planning for the civil works team.

One of the most critical aspects of the project was the setting out of pile caps for the Hyperia coaster itself. Working with structural engineers, our team provided accurate setting-out data to position foundations within strict tolerances—ensuring alignment with both the ride's design and safety parameters. By maintaining clear communication and delivering to tight deadlines, we supported the contractor's timeline through each construction milestone.

Project Outcome

The survey work we delivered was instrumental in supporting the successful delivery of the new Hyperia roller coaster project. With precision-driven outputs, we ensured the engineering team had clear and dependable data throughout construction. Our ability to adapt quickly to evolving site requirements, while maintaining accuracy and responsiveness, helped the client stay on track during a fast-paced and high-profile development.
